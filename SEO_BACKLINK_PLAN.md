# Western Beats — Backlink & Authority Score Plan

Your SEMrush Authority Score is 2. Authority Score is driven almost entirely
by **backlinks from other websites**, not by code on westernbeats.com. All
the on-site SEO work (new pages, schema, sitemap) helps you rank *once*
Google trusts the domain — but Google won't send real traffic to a brand-new,
zero-backlink domain no matter how good the pages are. This is the missing
piece. Everything below is manual outreach — no code, no deploys, just doing
it.

Target: get Authority Score from 2 → 15-20 within 60 days, which is roughly
where organic traffic starts becoming meaningful for a site this age.

---

## Tier 1 — Free, do this week (highest ROI per hour)

1. **Google Business Profile** — create one for "Western Beats Private
   Limited" at your Delhi/NCR address. Free, counts as a citation, shows up
   in Maps + local pack.

2. **Directory listings** (all free, take 10 min each):
   - Crunchbase (company profile)
   - Clutch.co (if you do client work)
   - AngelList / Wellfound
   - IndiaMART / JustDial (Indian business directories — these carry real
     weight for .in-relevant searches)
   - Music industry directories: SoundCharts, Chartmetric (free tier),
     MusicAlly directory

3. **Social profile backlinks** — every official social profile that allows
   a "website" field counts as a backlink: Instagram bio link (already have),
   LinkedIn company page, Twitter/X bio, Facebook page "About" section,
   YouTube channel "About" tab.

4. **Wikipedia citation** — if Western Beats or any artist you've
   distributed has a Wikipedia mention opportunity (e.g. discography
   sourcing), a citation link from Wikipedia is one of the highest-authority
   backlinks possible. Only do this if it's genuinely citable — Wikipedia
   editors remove promotional links fast.

---

## Tier 2 — Content-driven backlinks (2–4 weeks)

5. **Guest posts on Indian music/startup blogs.** Pitch a 1000-word article
   (you write it, or I draft it) to:
   - YourStory, Inc42 (Indian startup press — cover "free music distribution
     for indie artists" as a trend piece)
   - RollingStone India, Rolling Stone India digital edition
   - Wishberry Blog, Bandmuse, Indie Music Tribe
   - Reverbnation blog (if open to contributions)
   Each accepted post = 1 high-authority dofollow backlink + referral traffic.

6. **HARO-style press requests (Indian equivalent: Qwoted, JournoRequests,
   #PRrequest on Twitter/X)** — journalists writing about Indian music
   industry, streaming, or indie artists often need a quote. Respond with a
   founder quote + link back. Fast, free, high-authority links from news
   sites.

7. **"Best of" roundup outreach** — find existing articles ranking for
   "best music distribution companies India" / "DistroKid alternatives" and
   email the author asking to be added. Low success rate (~10-15%) but each
   hit is gold because it's exactly the keyword you want to rank for.

8. **Answer on Quora / Reddit (r/IndianMusic, r/WeAreTheMusicMakers)** —
   genuinely answer questions like "how do I distribute my music in India
   for free" and link to the relevant blog post (not the homepage — always
   link to the specific guide that answers the question). These are
   nofollow but still drive referral traffic and indirect SEO signal.

## Tier 3 — Partnership backlinks (4–8 weeks, highest long-term value)

9. **Every artist you distribute for** — ask them to add "Distributed by
   Western Beats" with a link in their bio/credits page if they have a
   website, EPK, or press page. Even 10 artists doing this = 10 relevant,
   contextual backlinks.

10. **Warner Music India Partner page** — since you have this partnership,
    check if WMI's partner directory or press materials can link to you.
    This single backlink would be very high authority given WMG's domain
    rating.

11. **Music tech / SaaS comparison sites** — sites like G2, Capterra,
    SaaSHub sometimes list music distribution tools. Submit Western Beats
    as a free alternative to DistroKid/TuneCore.

12. **Podcast guest appearances** — Indian music-industry podcasts
    (Inside Indian Music, Filtered Chaos, etc.) usually link to guests in
    show notes. A 20-minute conversation = a permanent backlink + audience.

---

## What NOT to do

- Don't buy backlinks from link farms / Fiverr "1000 backlinks" packages —
  Google penalizes this and it can tank the domain permanently.
- Don't do reciprocal link exchanges at scale ("link to me, I'll link to
  you") — looks manipulative to Google if overdone.
- Don't keyword-stuff anchor text across many links ("free music
  distribution India" as anchor on every single backlink) — vary natural
  anchor text (brand name, "Western Beats", "this distributor", URL).

## Tracking

Check Authority Score monthly in SEMrush. Faster signal: check
`site:westernbeats.com` backlink count in Ahrefs/SEMrush backlink
checker (free tier) weekly — you want this number climbing steadily,
not in one big spike (which also looks unnatural to Google).
